Frequently Asked Questions

What is BuildLog?

BuildLog is a field reporting app for construction and infrastructure teams. Capture daily work reports by voice, photos, and text — even without internet — and export verified reports your office can trust. AI analyzes every report for safety risks automatically.

Does it work without internet?

Yes. Save reports deep in the field with no signal. Voice recordings, photos, and text are all stored locally. Everything syncs automatically when you're back online.

How does voice reporting work?

Just speak naturally about your day. BuildLog transcribes your voice into a daily report you can review, edit, and export.

Can I export reports?

Yes. Generate professional PDF and CSV exports with photos, GPS data, and structured entries. Share with clients, managers, or regulators.

Who is BuildLog for?

Field supervisors, construction managers, infrastructure teams, and operations managers who need to capture daily work records quickly, reliably, and without depending on memory at the end of the shift.

Do I need training?

No. If you can send a voice message, you can use BuildLog. Most users are productive in their first session.
