# BuildLog Logo & Favicon Design Prompt

## Brand Overview
BuildLog is a construction site compliance and reporting mobile application designed for construction site supervisors, project managers, and safety teams. The app helps streamline daily reporting, ensure OSHA compliance, and document site conditions with AI-powered features.

## Design Requirements

### Logo Design
Create a modern, professional logo for BuildLog that reflects:
- **Industry**: Construction, building, compliance, safety
- **Technology**: Mobile app, AI-powered, digital transformation
- **Values**: Reliability, professionalism, efficiency, safety, innovation

### Style Guidelines
- **Color Palette**: 
  - Primary: #007AFF (bright blue - represents trust, technology, professionalism)
  - Secondary: Consider complementary colors like deep blue, orange, or construction-themed colors
  - The logo should work in both full color and monochrome versions
  
- **Design Style**:
  - Modern and clean
  - Professional yet approachable
  - Should convey construction/building elements combined with technology/digital elements
  - Suitable for both digital and print use
  - Scalable from large banners to small app icons

- **Typography** (if including text):
  - Modern, sans-serif font
  - Bold and readable
  - "BuildLog" text should be clear and legible at small sizes

### Visual Elements to Consider
- Construction/building elements: blueprint lines, building structures, construction tools, hard hat, clipboard, or construction site imagery
- Technology elements: digital/tech symbols, app icon style, modern geometric shapes
- Combination concepts: blueprint with digital overlay, construction site with tech elements, building blocks with digital grid, etc.

### Logo Variations Needed
1. **Full Logo**: Logo mark + "BuildLog" text (horizontal layout)
2. **Icon Only**: Logo mark without text (for app icon, favicon)
3. **Monochrome Version**: Single color version (for dark backgrounds, single-color printing)

### Technical Specifications

#### Favicon Requirements
- **favicon.ico**: 16x16, 32x32, 48x48 pixels (multi-resolution ICO file)
- **favicon-16x16.png**: 16x16 pixels
- **favicon-32x32.png**: 32x32 pixels
- **apple-touch-icon.png**: 180x180 pixels (for iOS devices)
- **site.webmanifest**: Web app manifest file

#### Logo File Formats Needed
- **SVG**: Vector format for scalability
- **PNG**: High-resolution PNG with transparent background (at least 512x512px for app stores)
- **PNG**: Various sizes for different use cases (256x256, 512x512, 1024x1024)

#### Social Media Images
- **og-image.jpg**: 1200x630 pixels (Open Graph image for social sharing)
- **twitter-image.jpg**: 1200x630 pixels (Twitter Card image)
- Should include the logo and be visually appealing for social media previews

### Design Constraints
- Must be recognizable at very small sizes (16x16px for favicon)
- Should work on both light and dark backgrounds
- Must maintain clarity when scaled down
- Should be unique and memorable
- Should not infringe on existing construction or tech company logos

### Target Audience
- Construction site supervisors
- Project managers
- Safety managers
- Construction company owners
- Professionals who value efficiency and compliance

### Mood & Tone
- Professional and trustworthy
- Modern and innovative
- Approachable and user-friendly
- Confident and reliable

## Additional Notes for Designer/AI Tool

- The logo mark (icon) should be distinctive enough to work standalone as a favicon
- Consider how the logo will look when used in the app itself
- Ensure the design is timeless and won't look dated in a few years
- Test the favicon at 16x16px to ensure it's still recognizable
- The logo should feel at home in both construction industry contexts and tech/startup contexts

- `logo.svg` - Vector logo
- `logo.png` - High-res PNG (512x512 or larger)
- `logo-icon.svg` - Icon only (vector)
- `logo-icon.png` - Icon only (PNG)
- `favicon.ico` - Multi-resolution ICO file
- `favicon-16x16.png` - 16x16 PNG
- `favicon-32x32.png` - 32x32 PNG
- `apple-touch-icon.png` - 180x180 PNG
- `og-image.jpg` - 1200x630 JPG
- `twitter-image.jpg` - 1200x630 JPG
