- Modern, clean, professional design
- Primary color: #007AFF (bright blue) - represents trust, technology, professionalism
- Works in both full color and monochrome
- Scalable and recognizable at very small sizes (16x16px for favicon)
- Suitable for construction industry professionals who need tech-forward solutions

VISUAL ELEMENTS TO INCLUDE:
- Construction elements: blueprint, building structures, construction tools, hard hat, clipboard, construction site
- Technology elements: digital symbols, app icon style, modern geometric shapes, tech grid patterns
- Combination: Show how construction and technology merge together

Professional, trustworthy, modern, innovative, reliable, efficient, safety-focused

DELIVERABLES NEEDED:
1. Full logo with "BuildLog" text (horizontal layout) - SVG and PNG (512x512px)
2. Icon-only version (for app icon and favicon) - SVG and PNG
3. Monochrome version (single color) - SVG and PNG
4. Favicon set: 16x16, 32x32, 180x180 (apple-touch-icon) - PNG with transparent background
5. Social media images: 1200x630px for Open Graph and Twitter cards

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
- Vector format (SVG) for scalability
- High-resolution PNG with transparent background
- Must be recognizable at 16x16px size

- Modern sans-serif typography if including text

The logo should appeal to construction site supervisors, project managers, and safety teams who value efficiency, compliance, and modern technology solutions.
